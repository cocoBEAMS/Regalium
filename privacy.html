Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information FriendConnect collects and how it is used.

1. Information We Collect

When you use FriendConnect, we may collect:

  • Basic profile information (such as name, bio, and preferences)
  • Messages you send through the platform
  • Technical data like browser type and device information

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve FriendConnect
  • Allow users to connect and chat
  • Maintain safety and prevent abuse

3. Messages & Content

Messages and profile content are stored so the service can function. FriendConnect does not sell private messages to third parties.

4. Verified Accounts

Verified badges are granted manually by admins. Verification does not mean identity has been legally confirmed.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Information may be shared only when required by law or to protect users.

6. Account Removal

You may request account removal at any time. Some data may remain for legal or security reasons.

7. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect user data, but no system is 100% secure.

8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated occasionally. Continued use of FriendConnect means you accept the updated policy.

9. Contact

If you have questions about privacy, please contact the FriendConnect team.
