Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information Regalium collects and how it is used.

1. Information We Collect

  • Basic profile information (name, bio, interests, age, etc.)
  • Messages, posts, and event participation
  • Community activity (such as joining groups or events)
  • Technical data like device type, browser, and usage patterns

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and improve Regalium and related features
  • Allow users to connect, chat, and participate in communities
  • Manage events, announcements, and news updates
  • Maintain safety, prevent abuse, and enforce rules

3. Messages, Posts & Events

Messages, posts, and events are stored to enable the service to function. Regalium does not sell private content to third parties. Event and community data may be visible to participants.

4. Verified Accounts

Verified badges are granted manually by admins. Verification does not guarantee identity, legal status, or behavior.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Information may be shared only when required by law or to protect users. Public community posts or event participation may be visible to other users.

6. Account Removal

You may request account removal at any time. Some data (like messages or participation in communities/events) may remain for legal or security reasons.

7. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect user data, but no system is 100% secure. Be careful with what you share publicly.

8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated occasionally. Continued use of Regalium means you accept the updated policy.

9. Contact

If you have questions about privacy, please contact the Regalium team.
